Mr Prasanna

Mr Prasanna Timothy is an old bangalorean. He is been teaching music for the past 20 years. His love for music started from a very young age. Though strived in different job roles, his passion for music still stays alive.

He started his musical journey by playing piano in chruch and leading the chruch choir. He worked as a music teacher in various schools and musical institutions. He has performed on many stages as a band member and a solo artist. His teaching method has brought a difference in many lifes.
